About Samitivej Hospital

Samitivej Hospital is one of Thailand's leading private hospital networks, accredited by JCI (Joint Commission International). Established in 1979, it offers cutting-edge medical services in areas like oncology, cardiology, orthopedics, IVF, and pediatrics. Moreover, the hospital is internationally recognized for its patient-centered care and multilingual support teams, making it ideal for medical travelers.

In addition to adult healthcare, the hospital also operates Samitivej Children's Hospital Bangkok, a state-of-the-art pediatric center specializing in newborn care, pediatric surgery, cancer treatment, and genetic testing.
